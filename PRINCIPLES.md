# SVX Principles

## Trusted Custody Without Surrendering Ownership

Users retain ownership while SVX provides custody.

## Security Plus Access

Security is useless without retrieval.

## Access On Demand

Important assets should be available when needed.

## Chain Of Custody Over Trust

The system should verify actions instead of relying solely on trust.

## Knowledge Without Leverage

Information and records should remain accessible without another party gaining unnecessary control over the user.

## Convenience Without Exposure

Users should not sacrifice privacy or security to gain access.
