# SVX Wedge

## Problem

People have valuable items they cannot afford to lose.

Most people rely on:

- their home
- a closet
- a storage unit
- a friend

These locations are vulnerable to:

- theft
- fire
- flood
- loss

## Wedge

Protect the things people cannot afford to lose.

## Initial Customers

- collectors
- small businesses
- inventory holders
- high-value asset owners

## Long-Term Vision

Create a trusted network for storing and transferring valuable assets.
